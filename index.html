MINERAL DEPOSITS ACCESSIBLE BY SURFACE MINING

  • STRAIFIED
    • HORIZONTAL SEAMS
      • THICK OVERBURDEN
        • THIN SEAM
          • BACK FILLING BY ROUND THE TRIP FORMAT(PARTLY BY DIRECT TRANSPORT)
            • * increasing overburden ratio *fixed depth *lateral advance *no cut off based on economic parameter
        • THICK SEAM
          • BACK FILLING BY ROUND THE TRIP FORMAT
            • * increasing overburden ratio *fixed depth *lateral advance *no cut off based on economic parameter
      • THICK OVERBURDEN
        • THIN SEAM
          • BACK FILLING BY ROUND THE TRIP FORMAT
            • * increasing overburden ratio *fixed depth *lateral advance *no cut off based on economic parameter
        • THICK SEAM
          • BACK FILLING BY DIRECT CASTING
            • * increasing overburden ratio *fixed depth *lateral advance *no cut off based on economic parameter
    • FLAT SEAMS
      • THICK OVERBURDEN
        • THIN SEAM
          • BACK FILLING BY ROUND THE PIT TRANSPORT(PARTLY BY DIRECT CASTING IF SEAM IS VERY FLAT -DIP =10 TO 20)
            • * increasing overburden ratio *increasing depth *advanced towards dip *cut offbased on economic character
        • THICK SEAM
          • BACK FILLING BY ROUND THE PIT TRANSPORT
            • * increasing overburden ratio *increasing depth *advanced towards dip *cut offbased on economic character
      • THIN OVERBURDEN
        • THIN SEAM
          • BACK FILLING PARTLY BY CASTING IF SEAM IS VERY FLAT (DIP = 1 IN 20) OR BY ROUND THE TIP
            • * increasing overburden ratio *increasing depth *advanced towards dip *cut offbased on economic character
        • THICK SEAM
          • BACK FILLING BY ROUND THE PIT TRANSPORT
            • * increasing overburden ratio *increasing depth *advanced towards dip *cut offbased on economic character
    • INCLINED SEAMS
      • GENTLY INCLINED SEAM(1 IN 20 LESSTHAN DIP LESSTHANEQUAL TO 1 IN 8)
        • BACK FILLING PARTLY BY ROUND THE PIT TRANSPORT AND PARTLY OUTSIDE DUMP
          • * increasing overburden ratio *increasing depth *advanced towards dip *cut offbased on economic character
      • MODERATELY INCLINED SEAM(1 IN 8 LESSTHAN DIP LESSTHANEQUAL TO 1 IN 3)
        • OUTSIDE DUMP
          • * increasing overburden ratio *increasing depth *vertical advanced *lateral advance for safety *cut offbased on economic character
      • STEEPLY INCLINED SEAM( DIP GREATER THAN 1 IN 3)
        • OUTSIDE DUMP
          • * increasing overburden ratio *increasing depth *vertical advanced *lateral advance for safety *cut offbased on economic character
  • NON-STRAIFIED
    • VEIN TYPE
      • NARROW
        • OUTSIDE DUMP
          • * increasing overburden/ waste ratio *increasing depth *vertical advance *lateral advance for safety * cut off based on economic parameter
      • WIDE
        • OUTSIDE DUMP
          • * increasing overburden/ waste ratio *increasing depth *vertical advance *lateral advance for safety * cut off based on economic parameter
    • MASSIVE
      • WIDE
        • OUTSIDE DUMP
          • * increasing overburden/ waste ratio *increasing depth *vertical advance *lateral advance for safety * cut off based on economic parameter
      • CIRCULAR
        • OUTSIDE DUMP
          • * increasing overburden/ waste ratio *increasing depth *vertical advance *lateral advance for safety * cut off based on economic parameter
      • IRREGULAR
        • OUTSIDE DUMP
          • *increasing depth *vertical advance *lateral advance for safety * cut off based on economic parameter
